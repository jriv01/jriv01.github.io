Spanish Club

    In Spanish Club, we:
  • Learn about the wide variety of people within the hispanic community
  • Discuss the social, economic, and politcal struggles within the hispanic community
  • Collaborate with cooking club in exploring latino culture by preparing hispanic dishes
  • Engage in hands on learning by painting pieces inspired by famous hispanic artists
  • Celebrate various hispanic holidays such as "Dia de los Muertos"

If any of these activities sound interesting to you, you should definitely join Spanish Club this year!!
